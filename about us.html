Who We Are

Hello! Namaste Everyone
Welcome to Pee Kay Orchard !

We are a family-run apple orchard located in the scenic region of Kandreri, Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. With generations of experience, we take pride in growing and delivering premium-quality apples directly to your doorstep. We are Located at Kandreri which comes under Nankhari tehsil in Rampur bushahr area of District Shimla and is about 110 km from Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. Pee Kay orchard is taken Care by my father Mr. Pramesh Kanain and my Family members. I, Sanjana kanain have made this website with the blessings of my deities and also my elders to provide you the freshest fruit at your doorstep.

Our orchard produces a variety of apples, including Royal Delicious, Red Golden, Spur, and Gala, along with fresh cherries and pears.

At Pee Kay Orchard Kandreri, our goal is to provide the freshest fruits while maintaining a sustainable approach to farming.
