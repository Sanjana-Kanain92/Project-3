About Your Visit

Discover the magic of apple orchards nestled in the heart of Shimla. Our guided tours and fruit-picking experience await you!
"Located amidst the serene landscapes of Shimla, SK Orchard offers you a chance to explore the beauty of nature. Walk through lush apple orchards, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and experience the joy of picking fresh fruits right from the trees. Whether you're a nature lover or simply looking for a peaceful getaway, our orchard is the perfect destination."

Explore Nearby Attractions

Discover the beauty and charm of shimla and its sorroundings while visiting Sk Orchard.

Shimla, Narkanda, Hatu Mata temple at narkanda top, Tani jubbad lake in kotgarh valley, Nankhari, Kotkali mata Temple at Nankhari top, Bhimlakali Mata temple can be a great options for you to explore.

What You Can Do

We offer a unique blend of nature, adventure, and relaxation. From exploring the beauty of our lush apple orchards to enjoying hands-on experiences, there’s something for everyone. The best season is Flowering when flower bud appears in the branches. Whole Orchard looks so incredible and Relaxing in the flowering season. So, its better to visit in Flowering Season. Here’s what you can look forward to:

  • Guided Orchard Tours
  • Fruit Picking (Seasonal)
  • Photography Spots
  • Tasting Fresh Fruits
  • Relaxing Nature Walks
